Neural Networks for Medical Diagnosis

Author: Dr. Rajiv Sharma

Published: March 15, 2022

Citations: 1530

Abstract

This paper explores the applications of neural networks in medical diagnosis, highlighting their potential to revolutionize the field. The authors discuss the current state of medical diagnosis, the challenges it faces, and how neural networks can be leveraged to improve accuracy and efficiency. The paper also delves into the various types of neural networks that can be applied to medical diagnosis, including convolutional neural networks (CNNs) and recurrent neural networks (RNNs). The authors conclude by discussing the future directions of this research and its potential impact on the healthcare industry.
